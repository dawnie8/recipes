Ingredients

    11 cups all-purpose flour
    2 tablespoons sugar
    2 teaspoons baking powder
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 cup milk or buttermilk
    2 large eggs
    1 cup melted butter

Directions:
   - In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
   - Whisk in milk, eggs, and melted butter just until combined.
   - Preheat a flat griddle over medium-high heat.
   - Scoop 1 cup of pancake batter onto griddle. Let pancakes cook until bubbles form before flipping.
   - Cook other side until golden brown. Serve hot with syrup.
